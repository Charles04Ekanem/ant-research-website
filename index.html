About ANT Research and Development

ANT R&D is a Nigerian-based research and development lab focused on Artificial Intelligence, Signal Processing and Embedded Systems. We aim to develop indigenous focused AI models, build smart hardware and provide practical solutions for local challenges.

Artificial Intelligence

Designing and training ML models, creating custom datasets and building robust speech & image systems.

Signal Processing

Developing algorithms for audio, speech and sensor data to improve clarity, anomaly detection and analysis.

Embedded Systems

Building intelligent hardware, robotics and IoT systems from concept to prototype.

Projects

CBAR

Clarity in Communication: Advanced speech enhancement employing four neural network architectures (CNN, BiLSTM, Attention, Residual Connections) and a custom Nigerian urban noise dataset.

MEDD

Multi-Engine Deepfake Detection: Deep Learning system with CNNs (InceptionResNet V1, EfficientNet-B0) and Transformers (ViT, Swin) for detecting anomalies in images and videos.

TT-GAN

Generative Adversarial Network: VAE-GAN for simulating Talinum Triangulare plant images, useful for training datasets and AI research.
